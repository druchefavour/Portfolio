CODING IS MY PASSION

Angus is a software developer and data scientist who has distinguished himself in delivering custom tools and applications that serve diverse industries. He is passionate about solving complex problems and working with big data.

His background as a business school graduate, with graduate degree in statistics and having led a distinguished reservoir engineering career in multinational oil and gas companies have given him advanced problem solving and leadership skills. Able to work well on own initiative and demonstrates high level of motivation required to successfuly lead and contribute in a team.

Angus is conscientious and able to deploy new skills. His software development specialty is full stack flex. He has carried out projects that delivered robust frontend and backend tools. Proficient in a range of technologies and methodologies including HTML, JavaScript, PHP, and SQL for full stack web development.

He has worked in and led teams that have used custom tools such as Spotfire, SAS and Design and Analysis of experiments to handle big data from oil field operations.
